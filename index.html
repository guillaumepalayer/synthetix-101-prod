The simplest way to access Synthetix is to create a wallet with Argent wallet. Learn why in our FAQ.

Download the app

Synthetix exists on the Ethereum blockchain, which uses similar technology to Bitcoin to guarantee that ownership and control of your tickets rest with you. To interact with the Ethereum blockchain, you need a wallet like Argent.

Transactions related to your Synthetix tickets purchased on Ethereum cost “gas”, which are micro amounts of Ethereum’s native cryptocurrency (Ether). Since most new users do not have Ether already, we strongly invite you to use Argent wallet that will cover your cost of gas while using Synthetix. (Using other Ethereum wallets may require you to pay for gas costs yourselves).

Frequently Asked Questions

What does blockchain technology mean?

Synthetix exists on the Ethereum blockchain, which uses similar technology to Bitcoin to guarantee that you own and control your trades. To interact with the Ethereum blockchain, you need a wallet like Argent.

Why the Ethereum blockchain?

Ethereum is a general-purpose blockchain with smart contracts that enable programmable tokens, like Synth tokens, which represents the synthetic assets in Synthetix.

How is this built?

On Ethereum, Synthetix is run by a suite of smart contracts that manage your balances and trades.

Synthetix smart contracts are audited and secured by different audit partners.

What is gas?

Transactions related to your Synth trades on Ethereum cost “gas”, which are micro amounts of Ethereum’s native cryptocurrency (Ether).

Step 4 of 5

Staking your SNX to mint sUSD

SNX holders stake SNX in return for fees from the Synthetix exchange and rewards from the system’s inflationary monetary policy.

1

If you have staked your SNX and minted sUSD, you are eligible to collect two kinds of rewards: SNX staking rewards, and Synth exchange rewards generated on Synthetix.Exchange.

2

Vest your SNX staking rewards in escrow. If you have staked your SNX and minted sUSD, you are eligible to receive SNX staking rewards, which you can vest here after they have been escrowed for 12 months.

3

Claim your rewards. You must be within 1% of the Target Collateralization Ratio. If you are below this figure, you will be blocked from claiming rewards until you burn enough sUSD.

Note: if not collected within 7 days, your rewards will be forfeited and rolled over into the fee pool.
